Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery options and areas?

We offer standard and express delivery options within Metro Manila. For cut-off times and covered areas, message us anytime.

Can I customize a bouquet or arrangement?

Absolutely! Use our 'Get In Touch' form to tell us your preferred flowers, colors, and budget.

What is your cancellation/refund policy?

Cancellations 24 hours before delivery receive a full refund. Quality concerns reported within 6 hours receive store credit.

How can I make my flowers last longer?

Trim stems at an angle, replace water daily, and keep away from heat/sunlight. Flower food is included.
